Developed by practitioners who understand the compliance challenges facing Australia's medical community firsthand, SafePost enables users to check social media posts, online advertising, and digital marketing content against AHPRA's advertising guidelines, TGA requirements, and Medical Board standards — delivering instant results in seconds.

> 'Every week, well-intentioned practitioners publish content that unknowingly breaches AHPRA's advertising guidelines. ## A Growing Compliance Challenge for Australian Healthcare

Australia's healthcare advertising landscape is one of the most heavily regulated in the world. AHPRA's advertising guidelines, the Health Practitioner Regulation National Law, TGA requirements, and profession-specific rules from the Medical Board of Australia create a complex, overlapping framework that even experienced practitioners struggle to navigate consistently.

Patient testimonials, before-and-after images, unsubstantiated claims, product endorsements, and comparative advertising are among the most common triggers for AHPRA investigation – yet all are regularly published by practitioners who are unaware they are in breach. The consequences can include formal investigations, conditions placed on registration, and significant reputational damage to the practice.

> 'The regulatory landscape is not getting simpler – it is getting more complex. Cosmetic surgery guidelines, higher-risk non-surgical procedure requirements, TGA advertising standards – each layer adds more for practitioners to keep across. No solo practitioner or practice manager should be expected to hold all of that in their head while also running a patient-facing business. That is exactly what SafePost is for.' — Rodrigo Martins, Founder and CEO, SafePost
